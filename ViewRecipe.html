Lactose Free Birthday Cake

Recipe Creator: Sara Ahmed

Details

Category: Lactose Free Cake

Description: Classic vanilla cupcakes suitable for all occasions.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix all ingredients.
  3. Pour into cups.
  4. Bake for 20 minutes.
